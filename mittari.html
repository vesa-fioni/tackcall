How to use

  1. Sail steadily on one tack and settle into your groove.
  2. Tap SET UPWIND MEAN to store your average heading. Do this on both starboard and port.
  3. The bar then shows how far you've shifted from that mean, and the tones tell you lift, header or time to tack.
The bar
LIFTLifted — you're gaining, keep going.
HEADERBeing headed; deeper colour = bigger header.
TACKHeader passed your threshold — consider tacking.
GROOVEWithin the dead band, right on your mean.
Compass

The large red number is your current heading. STB / BB shows the tack you're on — automatically or manually, set in Settings.

Sounds

Audio alerts play distinct tones for lift, header and tack. Turn them on or off in Settings.

Tip

Re-set the mean whenever the average wind direction shifts, or use "Reset means" in Settings to start over.
