Inertial vs compass vs GPS

A test bench that shows three heading sources side by side: compass, GPS course, and an inertial relative heading derived from the gyroscope, with the vertical axis taken from gravity.

Tap Anchor, then hold the phone still and tilt it: the compass swings, the inertial reading stays put. Rotate it flat and all three track. That gap is the benefit this proto illustrates.

This is a developer test page, not a navigation tool. It stores nothing and does not touch the Tackcall app's data. Synthetic mode shows a modelled, illustrative signal — not measured data.
